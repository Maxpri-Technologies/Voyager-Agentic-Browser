Advanced Browser Agency

01

Context Aware

Voyager doesn't just "scrape"—it interprets the live DOM and accessibility tree to understand the purpose of every button and field in real-time.

02

Native Interaction

The agent operates within your actual browser profile, utilizing your active sessions, cookies, and authentication to perform tasks without API overhead.

03

Traceable Reasoning

Every decision is logged in human-readable steps. You see what the agent sees, why it's clicking, and where it's going next.

The Execution Workflow

Voyager transforms natural language instructions into a sequence of precise browser interactions.

01

Instruction

Define your goal in plain text. Voyager analyzes the request and maps it against the current page's capabilities.

02

Reasoning

The agent surveys the interface, identifies required inputs, and plans a multi-step path to achieve the objective.

03

Validation

For sensitive actions—like payments or data deletion—Voyager pauses and requests your explicit confirmation before proceeding.

04

Completion

Once the task is finished, Voyager provides a full summary of the actions taken and the final state of the page.
